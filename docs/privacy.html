Privacy Policy — Big Square Keys

Last updated: June 2026

Short version: Big Square Keys collects absolutely nothing. Your keystrokes never leave your device. There is no internet connection, ever.

What data we collect

None. Big Square Keys does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage data, keystrokes, typed text, or any other information.

Internet access

The app does not have the INTERNET permission. This means it is technically impossible for the app to send any data anywhere — the Android operating system blocks all network access at the system level.

What is stored on your device

The only data stored is your own settings (which languages you have enabled), saved locally in Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services

None. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no crash-reporting tools, no advertising networks, and no third-party services of any kind.

Children

Because no data is collected from anyone, there are no special considerations regarding users under the age of 13.

Permissions

The app requests only the VIBRATE permission (for optional key-press haptic feedback). No other permissions are used.

Open source

The full source code is publicly available at github.com/pavlol/Big_Square_Keys. You can verify every claim in this policy by reading the code.

Changes to this policy

If anything ever changes, this page will be updated with a new date at the top. Given the nature of the app, changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue on GitHub.
